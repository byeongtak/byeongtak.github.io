About
I am a research scientist at Medical AI Co Ltd . Before then, I worked as a research scientist at VUNO Inc. I obtained my M.S. degree in Bioengineering at Seoul National University, where I was fortunate to be advised by Jung Chan Lee. Prior to that, I studid my B.S. in Mechanical Engineering at Pusan National University.

My research interest is applying AI to diverse biomedical challenges. I have experience in developing AI solution for real-world healthcare application. My research has been focused on addressing challenges in implementing AI solution to the real-world, such as domain generalization. Also, I led developing automatic ML experiment system. My work on developing automatic ML pipeline was successfully deployed, which has enabled medical staff who lack ML expertise to conduct various experiments with medical data. I would like to resolve various bioinformatics challenges. Specifically, I am interested in enhancing therapeutic targeting at both the gene or protein levels through the use of AI.
